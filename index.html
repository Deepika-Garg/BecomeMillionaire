Game Rules
Welcome to the quiz game. This game is inspired from "Who Wants to be a Millionaire" quiz show.

There are 12 questions in the quiz. You need to answer all 12 questions correct to win £1,000,000. For each question 4 answers are given. Click on any answer to select them. There is progress bar is shown in the right which will show your winning amount. With each right answer your winning amount will move up.

Correct and wrong answer sound has been added to enhance user experience. You can press that sound button to toggle on/off sound.

There are life lines called "50-50" and Audiance Poll are provided to help you with the answers. You can use lifelines only once. "50-50" will remove two incorrect answers and you will have only two options to select from including the right one. Audiance Poll will highlight the right only option.

Once you give the wrong answer you will be out of the game with your winning amount.

All the best to play the game. Hope you win the £1,000,000 and become Millionaire!!
